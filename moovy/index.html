•ㅤㅤAbout Moovy
ㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤToday, the number of children suffering from psychological problems (specifically autism and depression) is increasing, parents also spend more time at work, less connected with their children. Children with autism and depression also have their own interests and entertainment needs, watching movies & TV shows on smartphones is one of them. Children with autism or depression often have a very limited number of interests. However, at present, there is no application that meets the limit of content selection suitable for children and helps parents monitor their children. Moovy was created to solve that problem.

ㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤMoovy is an application that helps parents manage their children watching videos online, specifically for children with autism and depression. There, parents can rest assured that their children can use them while still being able to monitor, supervise and manage them; The application also supports children to express their interests, thoughts, and love.
•ㅤㅤFunctions
ㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤParents can choose to limit the genres of video displayed, excluding inappropriate content as soon as they create an account for their child.

ㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤMoovy plays videos, movies, and TV shows with permission and remote parental control. Every time they want to watch a video, the child will send a request to the parent's device, after reviewing the video content, the parent will decide whether to let the child watch it or not.

ㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤThrough watching and giving hearts to videos, along with friendly greetings, congratulatory words and recognition of results after watching the video, children can get acquainted, learn more about communication, language, social relations, expressing love,...

ㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤThe application also has statistical functions such as: charts of genres, movies, TV shows children watch by total viewing time or total hearts; The category of videos that children tend to like increases and decreases the most; ... through which parents, psychologists, and doctors can better understand and follow changes in children's interests, helping them easier to treat, talk to, and share with their child.
•ㅤㅤTarget users
ㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤ- Autistic or depressed child (~10 years younger) and their parents.
ㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤAt this age, children are still under parental control.
ㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤ- Children who have a hobby of watching movies and TV shows need a video viewing platform with appropriate content.
ㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤ- Parents who want to spend time watching online videos with their children.
ㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤ- Parents have to spend a lot of time at work but want to observe and monitor their children's interests.
