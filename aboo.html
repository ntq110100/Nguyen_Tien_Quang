•ㅤㅤAbout ABoo
ㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤCurrently, the demand for exchanging and buying old books is increasing day by day. However, there is no application platform that fully meets users' requirements for buying and selling used books. So we decided to create Aboo to address those needs. Aboo is an app used to connect book lovers who have a passion for reading, collecting books. Users will be both sellers and buyers. Old books are exchanged on the app quickly and conveniently.
•ㅤㅤFunctions
ㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤThe function of the app is to buy. exchange old books. Users can sell their books, trade or auction with others. It also have videos showing the condition of books will increase the confidence of people who need to buy books.
ㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤThe Discover function helps users discover selected related books based on their recent search or purchase history.
•ㅤㅤTarget users
ㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤ- Age: 16 - 25.
ㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤYoung generation, students, newcomers.
ㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤ- Have a habit of reading or love to read and collect books.
ㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤ- Want to buy and sell used books.

•ㅤㅤProblems statement
ㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤBuyers
ㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤYoung people have difficulty in buying, selling, exchanging, and auctioning used books because they have problems of not having time to find and buy directly, do not know where to buy, or rare books have been published for a long time.
ㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤSellers
ㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤBookstores want to sell or auction used books online but have not found a suitable online platform. Young people have many old books that they want to sell but do not know where to sell or to whom to sell.
•ㅤㅤSolution statement
ㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤBuyers
ㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤCreate an application of exchanging old books where people can easily find, buy or auction the books and series they want in the most economical way.
ㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤSellers
ㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤCreate an application of exchanging old books to help bookstores and individuals easily post for sale and open auctions of their old books.
